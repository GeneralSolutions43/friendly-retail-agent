# Product Guide - Friendly Retail Agent

## Vision
To provide online shoppers with a personalized, high-touch shopping experience through a friendly and intelligent retail agent that increases conversion and customer satisfaction.

## Target Users
* **Online Shoppers:** Individuals looking for a more interactive and personalized way to discover products and get support while shopping online.

## Core Features & Capabilities
* **Natural Language Discovery:** Users can search for products using conversational language, making it easier to find exactly what they need.
* **Real-time Logistics:** Instant access to inventory levels and order tracking status to keep customers informed.
* **Personalized Recommendations:** Provides style or usage advice tailored to the user's purchase history and preferences.

## User Interaction
* **Web-Based Chat:** Seamlessly integrated into existing e-commerce platforms for immediate assistance during the browsing process.
* **Messaging Integration:** Available on popular platforms like WhatsApp and Facebook Messenger to meet customers where they are.

## Personality and Tone
The system is designed to be configurable by the retailer, supporting three distinct personas:
* **Helpful Professional:** Efficient and focused on getting results.
* **Friendly Assistant:** Casual and enthusiastic interaction style.
* **Expert Consultant:** Sophisticated and curated advice.

## Business Goals
* **Drive Conversions:** Increase the likelihood of purchase by providing immediate, relevant help during the customer journey.
* **Boost Order Value:** Use personalized advice and discovery to help customers find additional items they'll love.
